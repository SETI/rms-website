     I used the calibrated profiles of Saturn's rings generated from Voyager 1 & 2 imaging mosaics contained in the PDS volume VG_2810_peer_review during Cassini SOI in July 2004.

     I found the data to be straightforward to interpret and well documented. I was able to easily interate the profiles into the software package we use to analyse Cassini images. When used in the analysis of imaging from the Cassini SOI sequence features from this data set matched with locations of features in the Cassini images.
