--- layout: base layout_style: default title: "The 2007-2008 Uranus Ring Plane Crossings" --- [ ![\[RPX LOGO\]]({{ site.assets_url }}urpx/urpx_logo_thumbnail.gif)](urpxlogo.html) # The 2007-2008 Uranus Ring Plane Crossings The Sun and Earth passed through Uranus' ring plane in 2007 and 2008, providing a unique opportunity to observe Uranus' rings and smallest satellites. The edge-on viewing geometry also permitted the timing of satellite eclipses and mutual events that can be used to improve ephemerides. ## Data Sets * HST data of the URPX event can be identified via [this OPUS search](//opus.pds-rings.seti.org/#/instrument=Hubble+WFPC2,Hubble+WFC3,Hubble+STIS,Hubble+ACS,Hubble+NICMOS&target=Uranus,Mab&time1=2007-04-01T00:00:00.000&time2=2008-04-01T00:00){:target="_blank"}. * The PDS Ring-Moon Systems Node would be glad to archive additional data of the URPX event. Observers interested in archiving data should contact us at [pds-rings@seti.org](mailto:pds-rings@seti.org) ## Tools * The [Ring-Moon Systems Node On-Line Tools]({{ site.baseurl }}/tools/) are available for visualizing the changing geometry and assisting with observation planning.
